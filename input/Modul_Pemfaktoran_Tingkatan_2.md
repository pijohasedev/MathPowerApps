# 📝 Skema Jawapan Lengkap

---

## Soalan 1 — Faktor Sepunya (2 sebutan)

(a) 8y, 12y
8y = 1 × 2 × 2 × 2 × y
12y = 1 × 2 × 2 × 3 × y
Faktor sepunya: 1, 2, 4, y, 2y, 4y

(b) 3w, 5w²
3w = 1 × 3 × w
5w² = 1 × 5 × w × w
Faktor sepunya: 1, w

---

## Soalan 2 — Faktor Sepunya (2 sebutan)

(a) 2b, 3b
2b = 1 × 2 × b
3b = 1 × 3 × b
Faktor sepunya: 1, b

(b) 6h, 4gh
6h = 1 × 2 × 3 × h
4gh = 1 × 2 × 2 × g × h
Faktor sepunya: 1, 2, h, 2h

---

## Soalan 3 — Faktor Sepunya (3 sebutan)

(a) 5bc, 2c², 3cd
5bc = 1 × 5 × b × c
2c² = 1 × 2 × c × c
3cd = 1 × 3 × c × d
Faktor sepunya: 1, c

(b) 4a²b, 8b²c, 6bcd
4a²b = 1 × 2 × 2 × a × a × b
8b²c = 1 × 2 × 2 × 2 × b × b × c
6bcd = 1 × 2 × 3 × b × c × d
Faktor sepunya: 1, 2, b, 2b

---

## Soalan 4 — FSTB (2 sebutan)

(a) 6h, 4gh
| 2 | 6h, 4gh |
|---|---------|
| h | 3h, 2gh |
|   | 3, 2g   |
FSTB = 2h

(b) 10m², 15mk
| 5 | 10m², 15mk |
|---|------------|
| m | 2m, 3k     |
FSTB = 5m

---

## Soalan 5 — FSTB (3 sebutan)

(a) 5bc, 2c², 3cd
5bc = 1 × 5 × b × c
2c² = 1 × 2 × c × c
3cd = 1 × 3 × c × d
FSTB = c

(b) 10m², 15mk, 5m
| m | 10m², 15mk, 5m |
|---|----------------|
| 5 | 10m, 15k, 5    |
|   | 2m, 3k, 1      |
FSTB = 5m

---

## Soalan 6 — FSTB Pemfaktoran

(a) 5e + 10
FSTB = 5
5(e + 2)

(b) 2ab − 8a²
FSTB = 2a
2a(b − 4a)

## Soalan 11 — Beza Kuasa Dua

(a) b² − 81
= b² − 9²
= (b + 9)(b − 9)

(b) x² − 1
= x² − 1²
= (x + 1)(x − 1)

---

## Soalan 12 — Beza Kuasa Dua

(a) 9m² − 100
= (3m)² − 10²
= (3m + 10)(3m − 10)

(b) 16y² − 49
= (4y)² − 7²
= (4y + 7)(4y − 7)

---

## Soalan 13 — Beza Kuasa Dua (dengan faktor sepunya)

(a) 3y² − 147
= 3(y² − 49)
FSTB = 3
= 3(y² − 7²)
= 3(y + 7)(y − 7)

(b) 5k² − 80
= 5(k² − 16)
FSTB = 5
= 5(k² − 4²)
= 5(k + 4)(k − 4)

---

## Soalan 14 — Beza Kuasa Dua (bentuk (x + p)² − q²)

(a) (m + 3)² − 16
= (m + 3)² − 4²
= (m + 3 + 4)(m + 3 − 4)
= (m + 7)(m − 1)

(b) (x − 2)² − 25
= (x − 2)² − 5²
= (x − 2 + 5)(x − 2 − 5)
= (x + 3)(x − 7)

---

## Soalan 15 — Trinomial (a = 1)

(a) x² + 9x + 14
Cari dua nombor yang:
Hasil darab = 14, Hasil tambah = 9
2 × 7 = 14, 2 + 7 = 9
= (x + 2)(x + 7)

(b) x² − 5x − 24
Cari dua nombor yang:
Hasil darab = −24, Hasil tambah = −5
3 × (−8) = −24, 3 + (−8) = −5
= (x + 3)(x − 8)

---

## Soalan 16 — Trinomial (a = 1)

(a) x² − 6x + 9
Cari dua nombor yang:
Hasil darab = 9, Hasil tambah = −6
(−3) × (−3) = 9, (−3) + (−3) = −6
= (x − 3)(x − 3)
= (x − 3)²

(b) m² − 2m − 8
Cari dua nombor yang:
Hasil darab = −8, Hasil tambah = −2
2 × (−4) = −8, 2 + (−4) = −2
= (m + 2)(m − 4)

---

## Soalan 17 — Trinomial (a = 1)

(a) x² + 7x − 18
Cari dua nombor yang:
Hasil darab = −18, Hasil tambah = 7
9 × (−2) = −18, 9 + (−2) = 7
= (x + 9)(x − 2)

(b) y² − 2y − 15
Cari dua nombor yang:
Hasil darab = −15, Hasil tambah = −2
3 × (−5) = −15, 3 + (−5) = −2
= (y + 3)(y − 5)

---

## Soalan 18 — Trinomial (a = 1)

(a) m² + 11m − 26
Cari dua nombor yang:
Hasil darab = −26, Hasil tambah = 11
13 × (−2) = −26, 13 + (−2) = 11
= (m + 13)(m − 2)

(b) k² − 8k + 16
Cari dua nombor yang:
Hasil darab = 16, Hasil tambah = −8
(−4) × (−4) = 16, (−4) + (−4) = −8
= (k − 4)(k − 4)
= (k − 4)²

---

## Soalan 19 — Empat Sebutan (Kumpulan)

(a) pq − qr − pw + rw
= (pq − qr) − (pw − rw)
= q(p − r) − w(p − r)
= (q − w)(p − r)

(b) x² + xy + 6x + 6y
= (x² + xy) + (6x + 6y)
= x(x + y) + 6(x + y)
= (x + 6)(x + y)

---

## Soalan 20 — Bentuk Khas

(a) 4(x − 1)² − 9
= [2(x − 1)]² − 3²
= [2(x − 1) + 3][2(x − 1) − 3]
= (2x − 2 + 3)(2x − 2 − 3)
= (2x + 1)(2x − 5)

(b) 9f² − 12f + 4
= (3f)² − 2(3f)(2) + 2²
= (3f − 2)²
